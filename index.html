Our Story

Made Different Because They Are Different

Every SOVA bag begins in a hotel — in the long-staple cotton sheets that once wrapped travelers in comfort. When those sheets are retired, most are destined for landfills. We see something else entirely.

We rescue these luxury textiles and place them in the hands of independent artisans who reimagine them through quilting, cut-layering, and meticulous handcraft — building a circular economy that transforms waste into value.

About Our Bags

Each Piece Expresses a Transformation

Our bags are made from 100% reclaimed long-staple cotton and designed with intention. Reimagined by artisans and finished by hand, each piece expresses a transformation from rags to richness.

Techniques like quilting, cut-layering, and minimal construction celebrate the textile's journey — not hiding its past but making it beautiful.
